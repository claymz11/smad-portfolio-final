Our Community Place is a non-profit organization based in Harrisonburg, VA that serves and provides a community for homeless and marginalized persons where they can work towards getting a sustainable job, affordable housing, or simply a hot meal. I had volunteered here when I was younger and chose them as the focus of a “call-to-action” design campaign by creating a compelling poster, web banner, and some products in order to raise awareness for their mission.
