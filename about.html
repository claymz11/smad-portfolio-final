Hello! I'm Clay, a designer and video content producer based in Santa Clarita, CA. I specialize in video/audio post production and web/graphic design. I received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Media Arts and Design, concentrating in Interactive Design, and a Minor in Music Industry from James Madison University in Harrisonburg, VA.

My passion is bringing your company, brand, vision to life through visual media, in whatever form that takes! Utilizing visual storytelling is your chance to communicate to the world your ideas or services. I'm constantly a student of all aspects of this profession, which is why you will find work that is focused in many different areas on my portfolio. I always enjoy learning and gaining experience in as many different forms of visual media as I can because I understand the power each one holds as well as their individual strengths and weaknesses.

Much of my educational background is rooted in online/interactive media, so I'm well versed in things like SEO best practices (Search Engine Optimization), social media marketing, user experience design, and website design. I'm here to help you navigate the online space however you need it, which has never been so integral to our culture as it is now.
