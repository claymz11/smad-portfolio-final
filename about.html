My name is Clay Zimmerman and I am a recent graduate of James Madison University in Harrisonburg, Virginia. I received a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Media Arts and Design, concentrating in Interactive Design, as well as a minor in Music Industry.

I'm mainly a web and graphic designer but I also enjoy videography and producing/performing live and recorded music.

During the summers of 2018, 2019, and 2021 I interned at Jenzabar, Inc., also located in Harrisonburg, Virginia, as a Quality Assurance Engineer the first year and then as a User Experience Design Intern the next two years.
